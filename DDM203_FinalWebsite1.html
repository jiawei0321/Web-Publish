Beginning

Pokok KL was founded by Datuk Sharon Haniffa and his wife, Datin Christine Bong. Both of them are the founder and co- founder of the Brickhouse Group.


Pokok KL started in October 2018, and is well-known for its gorgeous glass architecture, serving wholesome brunch set in the midst of lush greenery in the middle of KL. We provide a variety of scrumptious food ranging from appetizer to dessert, and even party props!
